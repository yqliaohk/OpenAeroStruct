Quick search

utils.py¶

Define the aerodynamic analysis component using a vortex lattice method.

We input a nodal mesh and properties of the airflow to calculate the circulations of the horseshoe vortices. We then compute the forces, lift, and drag acting on the lifting surfaces. Currently we can compute the induced and viscous drag.
